# Pedagogical Audit: Level 0 Curriculum (Modules 0.1–0.4)

This audit evaluates the content depth, pedagogical "leaning," and structural integrity of the Level 0 curriculum based on the Lurnava institutional standards.

## 1. Executive Summary: The Lurnava "Leaning"
The curriculum successfully departs from retail "trading" education and aligns with **Institutional Market Reading**. The core "leaning" is characterized by:
- **Clinical Observation**: Removing emotional bias and prediction.
- **Structural Priority**: Treating the chart as a skeleton rather than a signal board.
- **Mechanical Understanding**: Explaining the *how* (matching engines, liquidity) before the *what* (price movement).

---

## 2. Module-by-Module Depth Analysis

### Module 0.1: The Lurnava Roadway
- **Depth**: Introductory. Sets the "rules of the game."
- **Audit**: Excellent philosophical grounding. The analogy of "Learning a Language" vs. "Guessing a Secret" is a high-level pedagogical move that resets learner expectations.
- **Leaning Check**: 100% alignment. Explicitly anti-guru and anti-prediction.

### Module 0.2: The Market Is a Data Stream
- **Depth**: Medium. Moves from visual representation to backend mechanics.
- **Audit**: Strong focus on "Matching Engines" and "Liquidity Pools." This prevents learners from treating the chart as a magical entity. The introduction of **OTC** vs. **CEX** vs. **Futures** in the adaptations provides a depth rarely seen in "Beginner" courses.
- **Leaning Check**: Institutional. Focuses on the "Scoreboard vs. Game" analogy which is vital for professional detachment.

### Module 0.3: Candles Are Records, Not Signals
- **Depth**: High. This is a critical pivot point in the curriculum.
- **Audit**: Redefines the "Candlestick" from a pattern to a **behavioral record**. The focus on **OHLC Logic** (Commitment vs. Rejection) instead of memorizing 50 pattern names is the correct institutional approach.
- **Leaning Check**: High. The "Mechanical Wick" diagnosis in the Crypto track and "Quote Distortion" in Forex are "pro-level" concepts introduced early to build immunity to retail traps.

### Module 0.4: Timeframes Change the Story
- **Depth**: High (Refactored). Focuses on **Time Compression**.
- **Audit**: Successfully explains the "Zoom Level" hierarchy. The **Compass (HTF) vs. Microscope (LTF)** roles provide a clear workflow for the learner. 
- **Leaning Check**: Disciplined. The emphasis on "Mixed Evidence = No Reading" is the most important lesson for capital preservation at Level 0.

---

## 3. Pedagogical Strengths & "Wow" Factors

1.  **The "Unclear" Professionalism**: Throughout the modules, "I don't know" or "This is Unclear" is taught as a valid, high-tier answer. This is the hallmark of a professional program.
2.  **Asset-Specific Nuance**: The curriculum doesn't just copy-paste text. 
    - **Forex**: Focuses on Relative Strength and Spread.
    - **Gold**: Focuses on Safe Haven status and Volatility Spikes.
    - **Crypto**: Focuses on Liquidity Fragmentation and Liquidations.
3.  **Visual Logic**: The use of specialized visual keys (e.g., `timeframe-weight-ladder`, `wick-source-diagnostic`) ensures that the pedagogy is tied to high-density data visuals, not just flat text.

---

## 4. Areas for Potential Refinement

*   **Mathematical Rigor**: While "Pips" and "Volatility" are mentioned, a small bridge into **Position Sizing** logic (as a byproduct of volatility) could be hinted at in Module 0.4 to reinforce the "Sports Car" (Gold) vs. "Sedan" (Forex) analogy.
*   **The "Why" of Timeframes**: We mention *what* they are (compression), but we could double down on *who* uses them (e.g., "Daily candles record the decisions of central banks and institutions; 1m candles record the noise of retail and algorithms"). This adds institutional weight.

---

## 5. Final Verdict
The Level 0 curriculum provides a **premium, clinical, and disciplined** foundation. It successfully "deprograms" the learner from retail gambling habits and installs a professional **Evidence-First** operating system.

**Content Depth**: 9/10 (High for Level 0)
**Institutional Leaning**: 10/10 (Perfect alignment)
