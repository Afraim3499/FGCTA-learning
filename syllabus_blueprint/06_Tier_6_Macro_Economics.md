# Tier 6: Macro-Economics & Capital Flows

## Overview
Technical analysis (price action) is the steering wheel. Macro-economics is the engine. To truly understand why an institutional algorithm is pricing an asset higher, we must look at global capital flows, interest rates, and central bank narratives.

---

## Section 18: The Global Reserve (DXY)
*Goal: Understanding the US Dollar as the center of gravity for all global markets.*

### Module 18.1: The DXY Composition
- **Content**: What makes up the US Dollar Index (Euro dominance, Yen, GBP). Inverse correlation: When DXY goes up, why do EURUSD, GBPUSD, and Gold usually go down?

### Module 18.2: SMT Divergence (Smart Money Tool)
- **Content**: Using DXY to confirm institutional intent. If DXY makes a Lower Low, but EURUSD fails to make a Higher High, this divergence is a massive institutional footprint.

---

## Section 19: Central Banks and Interest Rates
*Goal: The ultimate driver of currency valuation.*

### Module 19.1: Hawkish vs. Dovish Policy
- **Content**: Translating Central Bank speeches. What happens when the Fed raises rates (yields rise, currency strengthens) vs cutting rates.

### Module 19.2: The Yield Curve
- **Content**: Analyzing the 2-Year vs 10-Year Treasury Yields. What an inverted yield curve signals for risk-on vs risk-off asset classes.

---

## Section 20: The News Matrix (High Impact Events)
*Goal: Surviving and profiting from volatility.*

### Module 20.1: The "Engineered" Liquidity Sweep
- **Content**: Why High Impact news (CPI, NFP, FOMC) almost always sweeps liquidity in the *opposite* direction of the true macro bias before running aggressively.

### Module 20.2: Interactive Task: The FOMC Simulator
- **Task**: `macro_news_simulator`
- **Mechanic**: User watches a simulated live feed of Jerome Powell speaking. Keywords flash on screen ("Inflation sticky", "No rate cuts expected"). The user must rapidly adjust their macro bias and place a trade in the `MiniReplayEngine` as the chart goes parabolic.

---

## Tier 6 Final Gate: The Macro Correlation Exam
- **Format**: User is shown DXY, 10-Year Yields, and EURUSD charts simultaneously. 
- **Requirement**: They must correctly identify SMT Divergence across the three assets and predict the resulting algorithmic draw on liquidity.
