# Tier 8: Elite Psychology & Mastery

## Overview
The final tier. You have the technical skills, the risk models, and the macro understanding. The only variable left to control is yourself. This tier builds the mental frameworks required for professional, institutional-level execution.

## Section 24: Deep Cognitive Restructuring
*Goal: Eliminating emotional attachment to money.*

### Module 24.1: Recency Bias and the "Next Trade" Fallacy
- **Content**: Why a streak of 5 wins makes you overconfident (increasing size), and a streak of 5 losses makes you fearful (missing the golden setup). Detaching the current execution from the previous outcome.

### Module 24.2: The "Need to be Right"
- **Content**: Retail traders want to predict the future. Institutional traders want to manage probability. Accepting that being "wrong" is just a business expense.

## Section 25: The Institutional Routine

### Module 25.1: Pre-Market Preparation
- **Content**: Scanning Macro calendars, identifying Killzones, mapping DXY SMT divergence, and defining the daily bias before the first candle opens.

### Module 25.2: Post-Market Journaling (The Feedback Loop)
- **Content**: A trade is not over when you close it. It is over when you journal it.
- **Interactive Task**: `journaling_simulator`
  - **Mechanic**: User is shown a completed trade. They must fill out a strict post-market review form, identifying emotional state, technical execution, and mistakes made.
