# Tier 4: Time & Price (The Fixings)

## Overview
Gold does not respect typical Forex Killzones. It is driven by the physical London Gold Fixing and the Comex futures open in New York.

## Section 12: The London Gold Fixing
### Module 12.1: AM & PM Fixes
- **Content**: The London bullion market sets the benchmark price twice a day. Institutional algorithms front-run these fixings, often engineering massive liquidity sweeps just prior to 10:30 AM GMT and 3:00 PM GMT.

## Section 13: The Comex Open
### Module 13.1: 8:20 AM EST Volatility Injection
- **Content**: The New York Comex futures market opens at 8:20 AM EST. This is the single most volatile time for XAU/USD. Understanding the AMD profile specific to this 10-minute window.
