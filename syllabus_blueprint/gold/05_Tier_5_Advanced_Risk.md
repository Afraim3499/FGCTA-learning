# Tier 5: Advanced Risk (Extreme Slippage)

## Overview
Gold is notorious for slippage. During high-impact news, your Stop Loss will not be respected. This tier teaches defensive sizing.

---

## Section 15: Managing Slippage
### Module 15.1: The Guaranteed Stop
- **Content**: When to use guaranteed stops vs standard stops, and how to account for extreme spread widening during FOMC releases.

---

## Section 16: Scaling out in Parabolic Moves
### Module 16.1: The Blow-Off Top
- **Content**: Gold tends to end major trends in a vertical "blow-off" top. How to scale out aggressively rather than holding for a larger target, as reversals are equally violent.

---

## Tier 5 Final Gate: The Flash Crash Crucible
- **Format**: User is subjected to a simulated 200-pip flash crash. They must have properly sized their position so that maximum slippage does not exceed their 2% hard stop.
