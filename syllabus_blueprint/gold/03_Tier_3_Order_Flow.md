# Tier 3: Institutional Footprints (Gold Specific)

## Overview
Gold institutional trading is heavily reliant on psychological round numbers and extreme, rapid liquidity voids driven by physical delivery demands.

---

## Section 9: Order Blocks at Round Numbers
### Module 9.1: The Psychological Magnet
- **Content**: How bullion banks engineer liquidity pools around round numbers (e.g., $1900, $2000) and how Order Blocks specifically formed at these levels act as titanium support/resistance.

---

## Section 10: The "Flash Crash" Imbalances
### Module 10.1: Geopolitical Wicks
- **Content**: Gold frequently experiences violent 50-100 pip wicks during news drops. Identifying which wicks represent institutional accumulation vs which wicks are pure inefficiencies that will be filled immediately.

---

## Section 11: Breakers & Narrative Shifts
### Module 11.1: The Safe-Haven Reversal
- **Content**: When a geopolitical fear narrative fails to materialize, the previous Bullish Order Block transforms into a Bearish Breaker as funds rapidly unwind their hedges.

---

## Tier 3 Final Gate: The Hard Asset Footprint Exam
- **Format**: User must correctly identify a Bearish Breaker formed after a failed geopolitical breakout and place a limit order at the mitigation point.
