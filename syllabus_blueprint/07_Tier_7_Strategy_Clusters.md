# Tier 7: Synthesizing The Model (Strategy Clusters)

## Overview
This tier takes every concept learned—Liquidity, FVGs, Order Blocks, Macro, and Time—and compresses them into highly specific, repeatable Strategy Clusters. This is where the student builds their specific playbook.

---

## Section 21: Setup Cluster 1 - The Liquidity Sweep & MSS
*Goal: The classic reversal model.*

### Module 21.1: The Anatomy of the Sweep
- **Content**: Wait for Asian High/Low to be swept during the London Killzone.
- **Trigger**: Observe a lower-timeframe Market Structure Shift (MSS) leaving a Fair Value Gap.
- **Execution**: Limit order at the FVG. Target the opposing liquidity pool.

---

## Section 22: Setup Cluster 2 - The AMD Continuation
*Goal: The classic continuation model.*

### Module 22.1: The NY Overlap
- **Content**: If London expanded upwards (Distribution), New York will often retrace into a London Order Block or FVG (Accumulation/Manipulation) to continue the daily expansion.
- **Execution**: Buying deep discount within the NY Killzone.

---

## Section 23: Setup Cluster 3 - The SMT Macro Divergence
*Goal: High-probability setups using the Dollar Index.*

### Module 23.1: The Divergence Entry
- **Content**: DXY breaks lower. EURUSD fails to break higher. Enter Short on DXY / Long on EURUSD at the subsequent FVG.
