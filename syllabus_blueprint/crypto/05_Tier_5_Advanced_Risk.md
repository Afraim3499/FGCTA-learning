# Tier 5: Advanced Risk (Perpetual Futures)

## Overview
Crypto volatility will destroy traditional risk models. A 10% move in an hour is common. This tier teaches survival in the Perpetual Futures market.

---

## Section 15: Margin Mechanics
### Module 15.1: Cross vs Isolated Margin
- **Content**: The lethal dangers of Cross Margin (risking your entire account balance) versus the safety of Isolated Margin (risking only the allocated margin).

### Module 15.2: The Liquidation Price
- **Content**: Understanding the math behind the maintenance margin and ensuring your Stop Loss always triggers *before* the exchange liquidation engine forces a market sell.

---

## Section 16: Dynamic Sizing in High Beta Assets
### Module 16.1: Adjusting Risk for Altcoins
- **Content**: If you risk 1% on Bitcoin, you cannot risk 1% on a low-cap Altcoin with 5x the volatility. Adjusting base position size dynamically using ATR.

---

## Section 17: Protecting Capital from Scam Wicks
### Module 17.1: The "Wide Stop, Small Size" Principle
- **Content**: In crypto, tight stops are liquidity fuel. We teach the institutional approach of using very wide invalidation levels but reducing position size proportionally to maintain strict 1% risk.

---

## Tier 5 Final Gate: The Liquidator Crucible
- **Format**: User must manage a leveraged ETH position through a 15% flash crash, ensuring their dynamic stop loss saves them from total liquidation.
