# Tier 3: Institutional Order Flow (Crypto Specific)

## Overview
Unlike Forex, crypto order flow is heavily visible due to public ledgers and transparent exchange orderbooks. This tier focuses on "Whale Footprints" and identifying where large players are defending prices.

---

## Section 9: Defense Blocks & Whale Accumulation
### Module 9.1: The Defense Block
- **Content**: In crypto, whales often defend certain price levels to protect massive margin positions from liquidation. Identifying these unbreachable walls on the limit order book.

### Module 9.2: Re-accumulation vs Distribution
- **Content**: How to read volume spikes at structural highs. Are whales selling into retail FOMO (Distribution) or preparing for the next leg up (Re-accumulation)?

---

## Section 10: Inefficiencies and Scam Wicks
### Module 10.1: The 50% Rule of Massive Wicks
- **Content**: Crypto is notorious for 10-20% "scam wicks" that instantly revert. Algorithms are magnetically drawn to re-price the 50% midpoint of these inefficient moves over the coming days.

---

## Section 11: Liquidation Voids
### Module 11.1: The Vacuum
- **Content**: When a massive long squeeze occurs, it leaves a "void" of liquidity on the way down. Price will often aggressively V-shape recover to fill this void because there is zero resting limit resistance on the way back up.

---

## Tier 3 Final Gate: The Crypto Footprint Exam
- **Format**: User must identify the 50% mitigation point of a massive liquidation wick on a live Ethereum chart and place a valid limit order.
