## LEVEL 2 — CORE STRATEGY FAMILIES

**Purpose**: Teach the 5 foundational strategy archetypes — the building blocks of all trading.
**Outcome**: Learner can execute basic versions of each archetype.
**Skill Level**: Beginner → Intermediate
**Prerequisites**: L1 complete

### Module 2.1 — Trend Following (Core)
- **Objective**: Enter and ride trends using smoothed price systems
- **Key Concepts**: MA crossover logic, trend confirmation, trailing with trend, patience vs. chasing
- **Linked Families**: TF_01 (MA Trend), TF_02 (MACD), TF_05 (Parabolic SAR)
- **Logic IDs**: TF_01.1, TF_02.1, TF_02.2, TF_05.1

### Module 2.2 — Breakout Trading (Core)
- **Objective**: Identify compression zones and trade the expansion
- **Key Concepts**: Range identification, breakout confirmation, false breakout awareness, volume on breakout
- **Linked Families**: BO_01 (Level Breakout), BO_02 (Session Breakout), BO_03 (Volatility Breakout)
- **Logic IDs**: BO_01.1, BO_02.1, BO_02.4, BO_03.1, BO_03.4

### Module 2.3 — Mean Reversion (Core)
- **Objective**: Fade extreme price extensions back to equilibrium
- **Key Concepts**: Overbought/oversold, band touch = revert, mean as magnet, reversion vs. reversal
- **Linked Families**: MR_01 (Oscillator MR), MR_02 (Band MR)
- **Logic IDs**: MR_01.1, MR_02.1

### Module 2.4 — Momentum (Core)
- **Objective**: Identify and ride accelerating price movement
- **Key Concepts**: Momentum vs. trend (speed vs. direction), momentum divergence as warning, relative strength
- **Linked Families**: MO_01 (Cross-Sectional Momentum), MO_02 (Momentum Indicators)
- **Logic IDs**: MO_01.1, MO_02.1, MO_02.2, MO_02.3

### Module 2.5 — Price Action Foundations
- **Objective**: Trade using raw price patterns without indicators
- **Key Concepts**: Reversal candles, continuation candles, pattern recognition at levels
- **Linked Families**: PA_01 (Candlestick Patterns), PA_02 (Chart Reversal), PA_03 (Chart Continuation)
- **Logic IDs**: PA_01.1, PA_01.2, PA_02.1, PA_02.2, PA_03.1, PA_03.2

| Adaptation | Forex | Crypto | Gold |
|-----------|-------|--------|------|
| **2.1** | 50/200 MA standard; daily/4H timeframes | Shorter MAs (9/21); 1H/4H; wider stops for volatility | Works all TFs; gold trends are slower but cleaner |
| **2.2** | London/NY open breakouts are highest-probability | 24/7 = adapt session logic; Asia accumulation → US breakout | COMEX open breakout; round-number breakouts are strong |
| **2.3** | RSI 70/30 standard; reliable in ranges | RSI 80/20 thresholds (extreme volatility); 3σ BB may be needed | Standard 70/30; gold ranges well in low-vol macro |
| **2.4** | Momentum rotation across major pairs | Altcoin momentum rotation (BTC.D based) | Limited universe; use Gold/Silver relative momentum |
| **2.5** | Clean PA on major pairs; low spread aids precision | More wicks/noise; focus on higher TF PA | Very clean PA; gold respects candle patterns well |
