# ACADEMY LOCK SUMMARY — L0 TO L3

This document serves as the formal control record for the locked states of the FGC Training Academy curriculum from Level 0 to Level 3.

## 1. Locked Levels
- **Level 0**: Market Foundations
- **Level 1**: Awakening
- **Level 2**: Mechanics
- **Level 3**: Order Flow

## 2. Level Breakdown

### Level 0 — Market Foundations
- **Module Count**: 5
- **Final Titles**: What Trading Actually Is, The Three Market Tracks, Price, Candles & Spread, Risk Basics, Platform Orientation.
- **Gates**: None (Manual completion).
- **XP Rewards**: 0 XP (Practice Level).
- **Unlock L1**: Complete all 5 modules.

### Level 1 — Awakening
- **Module Count**: 15 (5 Active, 10 Legacy/Archived).
- **Final Titles**: Why Price Moves, Market Participants, Supply & Demand Basics, Liquidity Basics, Timing and Discipline.
- **Gates**: Level 1 Knowledge Test (Single Gate).
- **XP Rewards**: 0 XP per module, 50 XP for Test (one-time).
- **Unlock L2**: Pass Level 1 Knowledge Test.

### Level 2 — Mechanics
- **Module Count**: 12
- **Final Titles**: True OHLCV Mechanism, Fractal Scaling, Institutional Price Feeds, Swings & Structural Points, Internal vs External Structure, Market Structure Shift (MSS), Range vs Expansion, Measuring the Range, Supply Zones Above Midpoint, Demand Zones Below Midpoint, Liquidity Targets Above Highs and Below Lows, Level 2 Map Review.
- **Gates**: Level 2 Knowledge Test + 2.12 Chart Map Mission (Dual Gate).
- **XP Rewards**: 50 XP per module (2.1-2.6, 2.12), 0 XP (2.7-2.11). 50 XP for Test, 50 XP for Mission.
- **Unlock L3**: BOTH Level 2 Knowledge Test passed AND 2.12 Chart Map Mission passed.

### Level 3 — Order Flow
- **Module Count**: 10
- **Final Titles**: Prediction vs. Confirmation, Candle Close Confirmation, Rejection vs. Acceptance, Retest Logic, Momentum and Displacement, Inefficiency / Gap Basics, Invalidation Zones, Entry Timing Checklist, Wait / Enter / Skip Decisions, Level 3 Final Scenario.
- **Gates**: Level 3 Knowledge Test + 3.10 Final Scenario (Dual Gate).
- **XP Rewards**: 50 XP per module. 50 XP for Test, 50 XP for Final Scenario.
- **Unlock L4**: BOTH Level 3 Knowledge Test passed AND 3.10 Final Scenario passed.
