# Crypto Strategy Vault: Trend, Momentum, and Price Action

**Module Overview:** This module covers the foundational Technical Analysis strategies. We teach all 52 strategies rigorously so the student understands the exact mechanical rules retail traders follow.

## Strategy Roster (Strategies 1-52)
*Every strategy below will be ingested into the `Strategy` database and taught in the Vault.*

### Trend Following (1-12)
1. **Moving Average Crossover (20/50 EMA)**: Captures momentum shifts.
2. **Golden Cross (50/200 SMA)**: Long-term bullish trend identifier.
3. **Death Cross (50/200 SMA)**: Long-term bearish trend identifier.
4. **EMA Cross with RSI Filter**: Trend entry only when not overbought/oversold.
5. **MACD Momentum Trend**: Trading the histogram and signal line cross.
6. **Parabolic SAR Trailing**: Following the dots for trend continuation.
7. **Ichimoku Cloud Breakout**: Trading the Kumo twist and Kijun crosses.
8. **ADX/DMI Trend Strength**: Entering only when ADX > 25.
9. **SuperTrend Indicator**: ATR-based trailing stop trend following.
10. **Alligator Indicator (Bill Williams)**: Trading the Jaw, Teeth, and Lips.
11. **Moving Average Ribbon**: Stacking 8-10 MAs to gauge trend compression.
12. **Hull Moving Average (HMA) Turn**: Fast responsiveness trend shifts.

### Breakout & Volatility Expansion (13-24)
13. **Bollinger Band Squeeze Breakout**: Trading the volatility expansion.
14. **Donchian Channel Breakout**: Buying n-day highs, selling n-day lows.
15. **Keltner Channel Breakout**: ATR-based channel trading.
16. **Opening Range Breakout (ORB)**: Trading the initial session volatility.
17. **Asian Session Range Breakout**: Capturing the London open momentum.
18. **Weekend Range Breakout**: Trading the Sunday night / Monday morning gap.
19. **Triangle Breakout (Ascending/Descending)**: Trading the apex compression.
20. **Pennant / Flag Continuation**: Trading the flag pole consolidation break.
21. **Cup and Handle Breakout**: Trading the rounded bottom continuation.
22. **NR7 / NR4 Breakout**: Trading the narrowest range bar of the last N days.
23. **Volatility Contraction Pattern (VCP)**: Trading contracting swings.
24. **ATR Expansion Confirmation**: Entering on surges in Average True Range.

### Mean Reversion (25-35)
25. **RSI Oversold/Overbought Fade**: Fading <30 and >70 extremes.
26. **Bollinger Band Mean Reversion**: Selling the upper band, buying the lower.
27. **Stochastic Oscillator Cross**: Fading <20 and >80 K/D crosses.
28. **VWAP Reversion**: Fading extremes back to the Volume Weighted Avg Price.
29. **Commodity Channel Index (CCI) Extremes**: Fading +/- 100 levels.
30. **Williams %R Reversion**: Fading -20 and -80 extremes.
31. **Envelopes / Deviation Reversion**: Trading deviations from the mean.
32. **Mean Reversion to 50 EMA**: Trading rubber-band stretches.
33. **Pivot Point Reversion**: Fading R3/S3 back to the daily pivot.
34. **Standard Deviation Channel Fade**: Trading the +/- 2 Sigma bounds.
35. **Gap Fill Strategy**: Trading the assumption that weekend CME gaps fill.

### Price Action & Chart Patterns (36-52)
36. **Support/Resistance Bounce**: Trading horizontal historical levels.
37. **Trendline Break and Retest**: Trading the backside of a broken trend.
38. **Head and Shoulders Reversal**: Trading the neckline break.
39. **Double Top / Double Bottom**: Trading the 'M' and 'W' formations.
40. **Pin Bar (Hammer/Shooting Star) Reversal**: Trading long wick rejection.
41. **Engulfing Candle Pattern**: Trading bullish/bearish engulfing bars.
42. **Inside Bar (Harami) Breakout**: Trading consolidation breakouts.
43. **Morning/Evening Star Formations**: 3-candle reversal setups.
44. **Three White Soldiers / Black Crows**: Momentum continuation patterns.
45. **Gann Fan / Gann Angles**: Geometric angle trading.
46. **Fibonacci Retracement Confluence**: Trading the 61.8% / 50% pullbacks.
47. **Fibonacci Extension Targets**: Trading to the 1.618 / 2.618 levels.
48. **Elliott Wave (Wave 3 Impulse)**: Trading the longest impulsive wave.
49. **Elliott Wave (Wave C Correction)**: Trading the ABC corrective structure.
50. **Harmonic Patterns (Gartley/Bat)**: Specific Fibonacci ratio geometries.
51. **Wyckoff Accumulation Spring**: Trading the Phase C shakeout.
52. **Wyckoff Distribution Upthrust (UTAD)**: Trading the Phase C trap.

## Interactive Simulation Engine Requirements
*   **The Charting Engine**: The UI must load a TradingView-style interactive chart.
*   **The Task**: The student is tasked with manually executing 10 successful trades using *only* the specific indicator (e.g., Bollinger Squeeze). 
*   **The Simulator**: The engine automatically grades if the student waited for the correct parameters (e.g., ADX > 25, MACD histogram positive) before clicking buy/sell.

## Pass/Fail Criteria
*   **Pass**: The student successfully executes 10 trades strictly adhering to the mechanical rules of the Technical Strategies. Profit is irrelevant; adherence to the rule is graded.
*   **Fail**: Entering prematurely, ignoring RSI filters, or failing to identify the exact Fibonacci level.
