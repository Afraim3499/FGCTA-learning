# Strategy Vault Blueprint: Master Index

This directory serves as the exhaustive architectural blueprint for the **645 trading strategies** across Crypto, Forex, and Gold. Absolutely no strategy from the original catalogs has been reduced or omitted. 

Every single strategy will be taught in Tier 3 (The Mechanics) and systematically exploited in Tier 4 (The Institutional Trap).

## Integration Overview
- **Total Strategies Documented**: 645
- **Crypto Strategies**: 206
- **Forex Strategies**: 201
- **Gold Strategies**: 238

## Core Objective
The purpose of this vault is to ensure that students are not just exposed to, but actively simulated on, every known technical, fundamental, and quantitative strategy. We do not skip the basics; we map every single retail and advanced indicator so that when the student reaches the Institutional Order Flow tiers, they know exactly *why* and *how* liquidity is engineered.
